# Upload Your Own Objects: Unleash Your Creative Power in Storyteller.ai
Ready to take your Storyteller.ai videos to the next level? Let's explore how you can add your own unique 3D objects to create truly personalized content!

{{ youtube(id="TphdnSNphQw") }}

## Why Upload Your Own Objects?

Sometimes, you need something truly special - something that perfectly captures your vision. While our built-in library is extensive, uploading your own 3D models opens up endless possibilities:

- Custom characters that match your brand
- Unique props that tell your story
- Personalized environments that set the perfect scene

## Step-by-Step Upload Guide

### 1. Enter the Studio
- Head to the Storyteller.ai Studio - your creative hub where all the magic happens
- Log in to your account if you haven't already

### 2. Navigate to Objects
- Look for the "Props & Objects" section in the menu
- Click on the "Mine" tab to access your personal collection
- Select "Upload Props or Other Objects" to begin

### 3. Upload Your File
- We support multiple file formats:
  - .obj
  - .glb
  - .gltf
- Simply drag and drop your file into the upload area
- Wait for the upload to complete

## Pro Tips for Success

### Optimize Your Files
- Keep file sizes reasonable for faster uploads
- Aim for simpler models when possible
- Use clear naming conventions for easy organization

### Best Practices
- Test your models before using them in important projects
- Consider the scale of your objects relative to other scene elements
- Back up your original files before uploading
