# Become a Master of Motion 
Want to take full control of your 3D objects? Storyteller.ai makes it super easy to move, rotate, and scale them with precision. It's like having superpowers in the digital world!

{{ youtube(id="l6DuhTl36DM") }}

## Why Move, Rotate, and Scale?

Imagine you're creating a video with a cool car. You might want to:
- Move the car across the scene
- Rotate the car to show it from different angles
- Scale the car to make it bigger or smaller

With Storyteller.ai, you can do all of that and more!

## How to Move Objects

1. Select the object: Click on the object you want to move
2. Use the move tool: You can find this tool at the top of the screen
3. Drag the object: Click and hold the object, then drag it where you want it to go
4. Use the arrows: You can also use the arrows on the move tool to move the object in different directions

## How to Rotate Objects

1. Select the object: Click on the object you want to rotate
2. Use the rotate tool: You can find this tool at the top of the screen
3. Drag the object: Click and hold the object, then drag it to rotate it
4. Use the controls: You can also use the controls on the rotate tool to rotate the object precisely

## How to Scale Objects

1. Select the object: Click on the object you want to scale
2. Use the scale tool: You can find this tool at the top of the screen
3. Drag the object: Click and hold the object, then drag it to make it bigger or smaller
4. Use the controls: You can also use the controls on the scale tool to scale the object precisely

## Tips and Tricks

- Keyboard shortcuts: You can use the "T" key for move, "R" key for rotate, and "S" key for scale
- Camera control: Don't forget to move the camera around to get the best view of your scene
- Practice makes perfect: The more you practice, the better you'll get at moving, rotating, and scaling objects
