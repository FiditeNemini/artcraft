# Search for Your Voice:

To begin with using [FakeYou](https://fakeyou.com/tts), you can search for and select from a vast library of voices ranging from fictional characters, well-known celebrities to more generic options and broad options to read out your text. Your options can be created through the use of Text-to-Speech (TTS) or by Voice-to-voice (V2V) conversion.

# Creating Text-to-Speech


1. Select target voice: Navigate for your voice by searching for a specific one or choosing a voice which fits to your liking. You can filter voice options by different available languages such as Spanish or German.

2. Input text: Input your speech text, make sure to write clearly and concisely for the best results during generation.

3. Generate Speech: Begin generating the voice by pressing “Speak” and be placed in the voice generation queue. Note that having a FakeYou membership allows you to skip the queue with priority generation. When the voice is generated, you can preview, download, or share your voice online!

# Voice-to-Voice Conversion

1. **Upload Audio:** Start by uploading an audio file containing speech that you wish to convert to a different voice. Ensuring the audio is clear and concise will yield the best results.

2. **Select Target Voice:** Choose the voice you want your audio to be converted to, like TTS, you can browse or search for the ideal voice. You may also record your audio directly in the “Microphone” section in order to create a clip yourself.
3. **Adjust Settings:** Depending on the voice uploaded, you may be able to tweak settings such as pitch control to create the best result fitting for the voice you are converting to.

4. **Generate Conversion:** Click the “Convert” button to process audio, after queuing (which may be able to skip with FakeYou membership priority) the converted audio will be available for playback, sharing, and downloading.
