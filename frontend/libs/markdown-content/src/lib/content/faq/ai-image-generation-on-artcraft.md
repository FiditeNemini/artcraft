## What is AI image generation in ArtCraft?

ArtCraft brings modern AI image generation into a creator-first workflow that runs locally. You compose your idea on a 2D canvas or in a 3D scene, then guide the model to render the final image with consistency and control. The result is a fast loop for exploration, iteration, and polished outputs.

## Core capabilities

- 2D Canvas guidance: sketch layouts, place references, mask areas, and refine with inpainting.
- 3D Scene composition: set camera, lighting, and subject placement for consistent multi-shot renders.
- Structure-preserving generation: keep the arrangement you designed while improving detail and lighting.
- Revisions that respect intent: re-run specific regions to fix details without losing the overall look.

## A simple workflow

- Plan the shot: define focal point, subject scale, angle, and background (2D) or block out a scene (3D).
- Add guidance: references, masks, or prompts that clarify style, mood, and key details.
- Generate and iterate: compare candidates, refine regions, and lock in the final composition.

## Quality and consistency tips

- Favor clear subjects and readable silhouettes; the model follows strong structure best.
- Use concise prompts; describe style, lighting, and subject specifics with minimal filler.
- For multi-image sets, keep camera, distance, and lighting consistent between shots.
- Iterate locally on regions that need corrections (eyes, hands, logos, or textures).
